Hello, my name is Andrew and i am a self taught artist based in Greece.

Growing up in a family with various members practicing different art forms i naturally developed an interest in image making through drawing that i did not tap into seriously until my early twenties. My background, training and first work experience was in Information Technology where i worked as a Computer, Network and Telecommunications technician. Eventually the two worlds crossed over for me when i was exposed to CGI (Computer Generated Imagery).

I found inspiration in the works of great artists whose efforts made it possible to create amazing worlds and creatures such as those of Star Wars and Blade Runner. Thanks to them i have developed a passion for creating digital environments that span from those that already exist in the real world to those that could never exist at all.

Today I have been working with computer graphics for most of the last 10 years with my area of expertise being 3D graphics. My work has been featured in TV-commercials, corporate videos, book covers, role-playing games, events and posters as well as behind the scenes with the occasional concept drawing or storyboard piece.

My skillset includes 3d modelling, texturing, lighting and look development, effects, rendering and compositing. Additionally my information technology background equips me with the skills necessary to write scripts and tools, automate procedures in addition to enabling me to work comfortably within a Linux environment.

If you've made it this far, thank you ! If you have an inquiry for a project feel free to send me a message so we can work together and bring your imagination to life.
